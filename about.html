Our Honourable Trustees

01 July 2023

Late Shri Nagindas B.Shah
Founder Member

A very humble person with caring nature. Who was born in Surat on Date 29- 03-1940 and having successful career in real estate business in Surat was always interested to do social service and do constructive work for society and established an English medium and Gujarati medium school in 1982 and successfully developed the Campus. After fifteen years of success in teaching students in Surat, handed over the School Management to a responsible management in 1997 and established Shree Amar Jyot Education Society in 1995 in Daman and started social service in Daman. After establishing it he always guided other members of management to develop educational institutes of Shree Amar Jyot Education Society and on Date : 25-02-2006 at the age of sixty years he left this world and is always blessing from heaven.

Honourable Devilaben N. Shah
Chairperson

A highly ambititious and hard working person was born in Surat on the Date : 21-04-1940 and started her career as Government Primary Teacher in Surat in 1960 and after finishing Twenty years of service, she wanted to do social service and hence she resigned from her Government service as Teacher in 1980 and started an English Medium and Gujarati Medium School in Surat in 1982 and in 1997 handed over the School Management to a very responsible management in Surat and as a Chairperson of Shree Amar Jyot Education in 1995. She started social service in Daman and is doing great social service by guiding other management members to develop both two campus of Shree Amar Jyot Education Society.
